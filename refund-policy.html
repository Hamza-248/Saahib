Refund & Cancellation Policy

At Saahib, we offer educational services with confidence in the value we provide. However, we understand that circumstances may change. This policy outlines the conditions under which customers may request a refund.

1. 7-Day No Questions Asked Refund Guarantee

Customers are entitled to a full refund within seven (7) calendar days of purchasing any service or enrolling in a course. This refund will be granted without requiring any justification, provided the request is made within the specified time period.

To request a refund, please contact us at saahib077@gmail.com and include:

  • Your full name;
  • Registered email address;
  • Proof of payment or enrollment.

Refunds will be issued to the original method of payment. Please allow up to ten (10) business days for the refund to be processed. Processing times may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.

2. Refunds After 7 Days

Refund requests submitted after the initial 7-day period will not be accepted unless required by law or explicitly approved by Saahib in exceptional cases. Saahib reserves full discretion in determining eligibility for any such exception.

3. Non-Refundable Services

Refunds will not be provided for:

  • Services that have already been fully delivered;
  • One-on-one or custom tutoring sessions already conducted.
  • Discounted or promotional service bundles;
  • Gift cards or vouchers.

4. Service Exchanges

Customers may request to switch to a different course or tutor within the first seven (7) days, subject to availability. Any differences in pricing will apply. Saahib is under no obligation to honor exchange requests beyond this period.
